Tortellini Soup

Description

This tortellini soup recipe has always been one of my favorite dishes to make
mostly due to just how simple and delicious it can be! It's also easily one of our
most customizable recipes! All you need is 15 minutes and some of your favorite add ons
to make this the most delicious tortellini soup you've ever had!

Ingredients

  • Tortellini of course!
  • Heavy whipping cream to make the soup creamy and delicious!
  • Chicken broth
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Seasonings:
    • Italian seasoning
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Garlic powder
  • Any add ons! this is your chance to get creative! Try adding:
    • Kale
    • Spinach
    • Broccoli
    • Anything your heart desires!

Steps

  1. Place an empty pot onto the stove element, set heat to high
  2. Place crushed tomatoes into heated pot
  3. Stir continuously for 5 minutes
  4. Pour in chicken broth to the heated pot until your desired consitency is
    achieved. More will make it soupier and lighter while less will make it
    thicker!
  5. Adjust heat settings to low heat
  6. Stir occasionally
  7. Add heavy cream and seasonings to taste
  8. Wait until our mixture reaches a boil
  9. Place in tortellini pasta
  10. Stir
  11. Wait until tortellini becomes plump
  12. Serve and enjoy!
