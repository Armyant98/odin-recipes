Udon Stir Fry

Description

This udon soup recipe will only take about 15 minutes to complete and will be
Absolutelt delicious! Its a great way to get some veggies in your food without
sacrafficing having a delicious dinner. Go ahead and give it a try! We are positive
this will become a household staple in no time!

Ingredients

Stir fry

  • 1 tbsp of vegetable oil or any other neutral cooking oil
  • 1/2 medium onion scliced or diced
  • 1 medium carrot chopped
  • 1 cup of green onion chopped only including the green portion
  • 3 cups of baby spinach
  • 14 oz of udon noodles per serving and either vaccuum sealed or frozen

Sauce

  • 1-2 tsp Sambal Oelek or to taste
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tsp of rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp of brown sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 tbsp of fresh ginger minced

Garnish

  • Green onion sliced and placed on top
  • Sesame seeds
  • Parsley or cilantro chopped and to taste
