Mac & Cheese Casserole

Description

This Mac & cheese casserole is easily one of the best options to feed a large
group of people with a tried and true favorite! This recipe is designed to serve
six people, but can be adjusted to however many people! Overall, this recipe
provides a quick and easy solution for when the family surprise visits, the kids need
a quick meal, or any sitaution where delicious food is needed!
